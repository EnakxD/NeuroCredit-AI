How NeuroCredit Works

01
Data Layer

Submit 14 alternative financial signals

UPI patterns, bill payment history, income sources, savings behavior — 14 signals that tell your real financial story. No bank statement, no CIBIL score, no discrimination.

02
AI / ML Layer

XGBoost model computes your score

Our gradient-boosted model — trained on 5,000 synthetic profiles mirroring India's informal economy — processes your signals and outputs a score from 300 to 900 in under a second.

03
Gamification Layer

Earn XP, badges, and level up your avatar

Your score translates into XP, financial levels (Rookie → Crypto King), and achievement badges. Every good habit moves you closer to the next level.

04
Blockchain Layer

Score minted as a Soul Bound Token

Your credit score is written to a non-transferable ERC-5484 SBT on Polygon. Permanently bound to your wallet — cannot be sold, gifted, or faked.

05
Privacy Layer

Lenders verify without seeing your data

A lender calls verifyCredit(wallet) and gets: score, grade, eligibility. Nothing else. Raw data never touches the chain.
